The problem

Democracy has a timing problem.

Policy is made every day. Citizens get a say every four years. By the time the next election comes, the decisions that shaped your life have already been made — without your input.

Periodicity

One vote, four years of policy

Between elections, governments legislate continuously. Citizens have no formal channel to respond until it's too late.

Simplification

One party can't represent everything you believe

You agree with party A on housing, party B on climate. Your single vote always feels like a compromise.

Access

Consultation nights are for the usual suspects

Town halls and public hearings are dominated by organised, vocal minorities. Working parents, young people, and newcomers are structurally absent.

Transparency

You submit a petition. Then nothing.

Citizens rarely see what happened to their input. The process is a black box. That's why trust keeps falling.

How P3 works

From proposal to policy — with your voice in it.

A complete participation round, from start to feedback, in six steps.

01

Government

A policy proposal goes live on P3

Local councils or ministries submit proposals with context, duration, and target group. A housing plan. A parking proposal. A new bylaw.

02

You — the citizen

Your app notifies you of something that affects your life

"New proposal: paid parking expansion, city centre." You open it, read the summary, and see where different parties stand.

03

You

You vote. Anonymously. In two minutes.

P3 knows you're eligible — not who you are. Your identity never leaves your phone. No email. No name. No trace.

04

P3 platform

Your vote is locked on the blockchain

Cryptographically sealed and burned after use. Impossible to change, impossible to cast twice, impossible to trace back to you.

05

Policy maker

The dashboard shows what 8,000 citizens actually think

Not a poll of 300 people. Real participation data, broken down by district, age group, and time — fully anonymised, fully auditable.

06

You

You see what happened to your vote

"The council revised the proposal based on P3 results." For the first time, you know your voice was actually heard.

Why you can trust it

Built to be unbreakable — and ungameable.

🔐

Zero personal data stored

P3 never knows who you are. Your digital wallet lives on your phone. There is no central database to hack, because there's nothing to steal.

⛓️

Votes are unchangeable

Every vote is written to a blockchain. No administrator, no government, no hacker can alter or delete it. The record is permanent and public.

⚖️

No one controls the outcome

The voting logic runs on open smart contracts. No shareholder, politician or P3 employee can influence results. The code is the referee.

🌍

GDPR compliant by design

Political preferences are special category data under EU law. P3 never stores individual preferences — only aggregated, anonymised results.

🔋

Energy-efficient blockchain

Built on Cardano's Proof-of-Stake consensus. Energy use comparable to a few households — not an industrial estate.

🔍

Independently auditable

The Netherlands Court of Audit or any designated authority can verify the integrity of every voting round. Transparency is structural, not optional.

Parliament 3 — the citizen chamber

Not just responding to politics.
Originating it.

Standard P3 gives citizens a voice on proposals made by government. Parliament 3 flips the equation — citizens set the agenda themselves.

The gap

Government still controls the agenda

Even with better voting tools, politicians decide what you get to respond to. Parliament 3 gives citizens the right to ask the question — not just answer it.

How it works

Citizens open a topic. The community builds it.

Anyone can raise a societal issue. Others respond, refine, upvote. The best insights rise to the top through collective intelligence — no editor, no agenda.

Quality filter

Top contributors decide when it's ready

When a topic is sufficiently developed, the most active contributors vote. At 70% approval, the proposal travels via smart contract directly to the relevant government body.

The result

A citizen proposal that's hard to ignore

Not a petition. A fully argued, community-validated proposal with participation data — certified on blockchain and delivered directly to parliament or municipality.

What politicians can do,
citizens can do too.

P3 doesn't replace your elected representatives. It puts citizens on equal footing — able to propose, not just respond. Results remain advisory. Decisions stay with elected bodies.
